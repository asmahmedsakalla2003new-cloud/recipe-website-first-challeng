Simple Fattoush Recipe

A quick and easy dish, perfect for any meal. This combines a variety of vegetables with water-soaked bread in a perfectly prepared dish, and can be garnished with parsley and nuts.

Preparation time

Total: Approximately 10 minutes
Preparation: 10 minutes
Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 4-5 radishes, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 2 Arabic pita bread, cut into small pieces
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash and chop all the vegetables (lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, radishes, and bell pepper) into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Chop the fresh parsley and mint, then add them to the vegetables.
  3. Cut the Arabic pita bread into small pieces and toast or fry them until crispy.
  4. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, sumac, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. In a large salad bowl, combine the vegetables, herbs, and dressing.
  6. Add the crispy pita bread pieces just before serving to keep them crunchy.
  7. Optional: Garnish with pomegranate seeds for extra flavor and color.
